Electric Bike

A custom electric bike conversion project featuring a 48V motor system, regenerative braking, and smart battery management. Built from scratch with custom PCB design and 3D printed components.

Key Features

  • 48V 1000W brushless motor system
  • Regenerative braking with energy recovery
  • Custom BMS (Battery Management System)
  • LCD display with speed, battery, and trip data
  • Smartphone integration via Bluetooth
  • 3D printed motor mount and accessories

Technologies Used

KiCad PCB Design Fusion 360 3D Printing Arduino Electrical Engineering Lithium Battery Tech
