Racial Representation in Math Classes

An in-depth statistical analysis of Cambridge Rindge & Latin School (CRLS) enrollment data, exploring how different student groups are distributed across levels of math coursework.

1. Project Overview

This project analyzes the racial demographics of math classes at CRLS to identify potential disparities. By examining enrollment data, I aimed to uncover patterns in student distribution across different course levels.

2. Methodology

I utilized Python along with Pandas for data manipulation, and Matplotlib and Scipy for statistical visualization and testing. The analysis involved cleaning the enrollment data and performing various statistical tests to determine significant differences in representation.

3. Research Paper

The full findings, methodology, and statistical rigor are detailed in the attached 36-page thesis paper.
