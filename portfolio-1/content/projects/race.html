Comparative Analysis of Racial Representation in Math Classes

I completed an in-depth analysis of Cambridge Rindge & Latin School (CRLS) enrollment data. That work revealed several notable patterns in how different student groups are distributed across levels of math coursework. Building on those findings, I plan to expand the dataset to include comparable data from other districts in Massachusetts so that we can contextualize CRLS’s profile within a broader landscape.
