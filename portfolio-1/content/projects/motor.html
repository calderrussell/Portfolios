3D Designed and Printed Motor

I created this motor for final project in my electricity and magnatism class. I 3d modeled the entire thing and then self wound the stator. As part of the final project I had to measeure the KV and test what voltage it could gereate when spinning at different RPMs.

Key Features

  • Custom 3D design tailored for optimal performance
  • Self-wound stator for improved efficiency
  • KV measurement for precise speed control
  • Testing capabilities for various RPMs
  • Compact and lightweight design

Technologies Used

Fusion 360 E&M Raspberry Pi 3D Printing
